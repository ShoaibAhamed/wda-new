RCP is the leading body for physicians
in the UK and internationally.

Founded in 1518 by a Royal Charter from King Henry VIII, the Royal College of Physicians of London (RCP) is the oldest medical college in England. The RCP is a professional membership body for physicians, with 34,000 members and fellows across the globe.

As the leading body for physicians in the UK and internationally, the Royal College of Physicians works to achieve their vision by influencing the way that healthcare is designed and delivered, promoting good health and leading the prevention of ill health across communities, and supporting physicians to fulfill their potential.
